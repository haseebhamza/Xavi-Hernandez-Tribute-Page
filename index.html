Xavi Hernández

Greatest Midfielder of all Time




Different Pictures of Xavi

Xavier Hernández known as Xavi, is a Spanish professional football manager and former FC Barcelona player who is the manager of Qatar Stars League club Al Sadd. Widely considered as one of the best midfielders in the history of the sport and one of the greatest Spanish players of all time, Xavi is renowned for his passing, ball retention and positioning.


Here's a timeline of Xavi's Achievements with FC Barcelona and Spanish National Team:


  • 1980 - Born in Terrassa, Barcelona.

  • 1998 - Made first team appearance for FC Barcelona.

  • 2000 - Made debut in the Spanish National Team.

  • 2004 - Became the vice-captain of FC Barcelona.

  • 2005 - Named Laliga Spanish Player of the Year.

  • 2006 - Won Laliga and UEFA Champions League with FC Barcelona.

  • 2008 - Won the UEFA Euro Championship and was named as Player of the Tournament.

  • 2009 - Won the historic Treble and later on Sextuple with FC Barcelona and was named as UEFA Midfielder of the Year.

  • 2010 - Won the FIFA World Cup with Spain and was Man of the Match against Germany in the Semifinal.

  • 2011 - Became the player with most appearances in the history of FC Barcelona, and won Laliga and UEFA Champions League.

  • 2012 - Won the UEFA Euro Championship with Spain again and became the first player to have assisted in 2 European Championship Finals.

  • 2014 - Retired as the most successful footballer in the history of Spain after the FIFA World Cup.

  • 2015 - Announced retirement after captaining FC Barcelona to historic 2nd Treble (most in history by any club).
